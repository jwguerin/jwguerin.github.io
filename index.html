Welcome to my resume webpage. I am interested in becoming a front-end developer and eventually moving into UX and web design. I believe this aligns with my service and tech-focused work experience and educational background.

My interest in technology started as a child. When I was 7 years old, my parents got their first desktop computer. I didn't know much about it at first, but knew I was interested in working with technology from that point. I always enjoyed building when I grew up. I built all the lego sets money could buy, as well as K'nex, Lincoln Logs and everything in between. As I got older, I taught myself more about computers until I went to college and majored in Business Information Technology. From here I reaffirmed my interest in anything computer related.

My favorite classes were:

  • Wed Based Decision Support Systems
  • Database Design
  • Advanced Data Modeling

I went on to graduate with Cum Laude distinction (0.02 GPA away from Magna Cum Laude) and secure my first 'real job' in a desktop support role at the Depository Trust & Celaring Corporation (DTCC). I've since discovered I want to do something where I create rather than support, where I can use my creativity to produce and design software, webpages or applications.
